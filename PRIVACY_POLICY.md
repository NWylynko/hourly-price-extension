### Privacy Policy for "Hourly Wage Price Converter" Chrome Extension

#### Last Updated: 10/4/2024

At "Hourly Wage Price Converter," we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information we collect, how it's used, and the steps we take to safeguard your information. By using the "Hourly Wage Price Converter" Chrome Extension, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

#### Information Collection and Use

The "Hourly Wage Price Converter" Chrome Extension is designed to help you understand how many hours you need to work to afford products or services online by converting displayed prices into equivalent hours based on your hourly wage. To enable this functionality, we require you to input your hourly wage into the extension.

- **Personal Information:** The only piece of personal information we collect is the hourly wage rate you enter into the extension. This information is solely used to perform the conversion calculation and is not used for any other purpose.
  
- **Storage:** Your hourly wage rate is stored locally on your computer using the Chrome Storage API. We do not send or store this information on any external servers or services.

#### Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your personal information with any third parties. Since the extension operates entirely offline and does not communicate with external servers, your data remains on your device and is under your control.

#### Data Security

We are committed to ensuring the security of your information. While the "Hourly Wage Price Converter" Chrome Extension stores your hourly wage data locally on your device, we encourage you to take steps to protect your device against unauthorized access.
